Welcome to my website. I am an [Assistant Researcher at Centro de Investigaciones del Mar y la Atmósfera](http://www.cima.fcen.uba.ar/personal.php?p=I) at the University of Buenos Aires, Argentina. I hold PhD and Diploma degrees from the Department of Meteorology at the University of Buenos Aires.

My [research](https://marisolosman.github.io/research/) mainly focuses on variability, predictability and prediction skill on interannual and decadal timescales.
